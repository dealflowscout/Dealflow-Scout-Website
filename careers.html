How we work

Culture isn't a perk. It's how we get things done.

We're small by design. Every person at Dealflow Scout owns something that matters — and has the context and autonomy to make it great.

Ownership over process

We don't have product managers standing between engineers and customers. We don't have layers of approval for design decisions. If you own something, you're responsible for understanding the problem, exploring solutions, and shipping something that works. That's the whole job.

Default to writing

We're async-first, which means we write things down. Project briefs, meeting notes, decision logs — everything important is documented. We think writing is a forcing function for clear thinking, and we make space for it.

Customer obsession

Every engineer, designer, and data scientist has access to customer calls, support tickets, and usage data. We believe proximity to the customer is a competitive advantage, not just a sales responsibility.

High bar, high trust

We hire slowly and fire fast. Not because we're ruthless — but because a small team only works when every person is excellent and trustworthy. We invest heavily in each person we bring in, and we expect the same back.

Ready to build something that matters?

We read every application. If you're great, we'll find a way to work with you.
