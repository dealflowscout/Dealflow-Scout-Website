FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?
Start with a full 14-day trial of the Growth plan — no credit card required. You'll get access to all features including Scout AI, unlimited profiles, and integrations. At the end of the trial, choose the plan that fits your team.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account settings. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we prorate the difference. If you downgrade, changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
What counts as a "Scout AI query"?
A query is any request sent to Scout AI — searching for companies, generating research reports, drafting outreach, or asking market questions. Growth and Enterprise plans have unlimited queries so you can move fast without counting.
Do you offer discounts for early-stage funds?
Yes. We offer a 30% discount for emerging fund managers raising their first or second fund under $50M AUM. Reach out to our team and we'll get you set up quickly.
How is data sourced and how fresh is it?
Dealflow Scout aggregates data daily from SEC EDGAR, Product Hunt, GitHub, YC, Techstars, 500 Global, and GDELT news. Company profiles are refreshed every 24 hours. Thesis scoring is recalculated whenever new signals appear.
Is Dealflow Scout SOC 2 certified?
Yes. We are SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified. Enterprise customers receive full compliance documentation and can request a copy of our latest audit report under NDA.
Can I use Dealflow Scout with our existing CRM?
Growth and Enterprise plans include native integrations with Affinity, Salesforce, and HubSpot. We also offer a Zapier connector and n8n node for any CRM that supports webhooks. The API is available on Enterprise for custom integrations.
What is the Enterprise onboarding process?
Enterprise customers get a dedicated onboarding specialist who will configure your investment thesis, set up data integrations, train your team, and ensure you're getting signal from day one — typically within 5 business days.
