Our mission

Making venture capital a level playing field

The venture capital industry has always run on relationships and pattern matching. For decades, the best deals went to the firms with the deepest networks — not necessarily the ones with the sharpest thesis or the fastest research process.

We're changing that. Dealflow Scout gives any VC firm — from a $10M emerging fund to a $5B multi-stage platform — access to the same quality of signal, the same real-time intelligence, and the same AI-powered deal discovery that was once reserved for firms with armies of analysts.

The future of venture capital is data-driven, thesis-first, and open to anyone who's willing to build conviction on the right information.

Our values

What we believe

These aren't posters on the wall. They're the principles that shape every product decision, every hire, and every customer conversation.

🎯

Signal over noise

The venture world is drowning in data but starving for insight. We obsess over the quality and relevance of every signal we surface — because a weak signal acted on is worse than no signal at all. We'd rather show you three great companies than thirty mediocre ones.

⚡

Speed is alpha

In venture, information advantage decays fast. The best investors move with conviction early — and our job is to get them there first. We build every feature with the question: "Does this help an investor act faster, with more confidence?" If the answer is no, we don't build it.

🔒

Trust is infrastructure

Investors share their investment thesis, portfolio data, and sourcing strategies with us. Founders trust us with their company's trajectory and future plans. We treat that trust as sacred infrastructure — something we build on, never something we exploit.
